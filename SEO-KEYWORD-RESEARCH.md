### Summer (December - February) - PEAK SEASON
**Highest Search Volume Pests:**
- Cockroaches (seek cool, damp areas)
- Termites (swarm season)
- Mosquitoes
- Ants
- Wasps/bees
- Flies

**Target Keywords:**
- summer pest control sydney
- cockroach infestation sydney summer
- termite swarm season sydney

### Autumn (March - May)
**Increasing Searches:**
- Rodents (start seeking shelter)
- Spiders (moving indoors)
- Cockroaches

**Target Keywords:**

**Target Keywords:**

**Prevention Season:**
- Termite inspections (before swarm)
- General pest prevention
- Spider treatments

**Target Keywords:**
- spring pest control sydney
