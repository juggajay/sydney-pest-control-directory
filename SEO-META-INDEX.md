## POWER WORDS USED (And Why)

### Trust Signals
- **EPA Licensed** - Legal authority
- **Professional** - Quality indicator
- **Verified** - Validation signal

### Urgency
- **24 hours / Same-day** - Time pressure
- **Fast / Immediate** - Action urgency
- **Today** - Now vs later

### Social Proof
- **4.8★ / Top-rated** - Rating signal
- **100+ / 23+ operators** - Scale
- **47 reviews** - Review count
- **12+ years** - Experience

### Action CTAs
- **Get** - Direct benefit
- **Request** - Polite action
- **Compare** - Decision-making
- **Find** - Search intent match

### Reassurance
- **Free quotes** - No commitment
- **Transparent pricing** - No surprises
- **No hidden fees** - Honesty signal
