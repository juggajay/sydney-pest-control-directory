## 10. KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Schema markup is our biggest quick win** - Safe Pest has NONE, others are incomplete

2. **Suburb pages are critical** - Flick has 40+, most have <10. Target 20-30 pages

3. **Reviews drive conversions** - Pest2Kill dominates with 1000+. Target 100+ in 6 months

4. **Content gaps are massive** - No one does video, interactive tools, or deep comparisons

5. **Directories can't be outranked** for generic terms - Focus on long-tail and local

6. **Trust signals matter** - Guarantees, years, certifications, social proof

7. **Local backlinks are underutilized** - Sponsorships and partnerships are open
