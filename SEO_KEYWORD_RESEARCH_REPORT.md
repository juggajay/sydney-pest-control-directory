## 11. TECHNICAL SEO RECOMMENDATIONS

### On-Page Optimization
- Title Tags: "[Service] [Location] | [Brand] - [USP]"
  - Example: "Pest Control Sydney | ABC Pest - 24/7 Emergency Service"
- Meta Descriptions: Include service, location, USP, and CTA (155-160 characters)
- H1: One per page, include primary keyword
- H2-H6: Use for service types, suburbs, FAQs
- Image Alt Text: Descriptive with keywords where natural
- URL Structure: /service-location/ (e.g., /termite-inspection-sydney/)

### Schema Markup (Structured Data)
- LocalBusiness schema
- Service schema for each pest type
- Review schema
- FAQ schema for question pages
- BreadcrumbList schema

### Local SEO Critical Elements
- Google Business Profile fully optimized
- Consistent NAP across all citations
- Local business directories (True Local, Yelp, etc.)
- Industry directories (AEPMA, Pest2Kill, etc.)
- Suburb-specific GBP posts
- Regular review generation

### Mobile Optimization
